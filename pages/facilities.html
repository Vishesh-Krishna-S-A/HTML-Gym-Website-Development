Our Facilities

At Velocity Fit, we offer a range of state-of-the-art facilities to ensure that every workout is a step toward achieving your fitness goals. Our gym is designed to cater to all your fitness needs, from strength training to relaxation and recovery.

  1. Workout Areas
    Our gym features dedicated workout areas, designed to help you get the most out of every session:

    • Cardio Zone: Equipped with the latest treadmills, bikes, and rowing machines, this area is perfect for getting your heart rate up and burning calories.
    • Strength Training Zone: Featuring a wide variety of free weights, dumbbells, and barbells, this area is ideal for building muscle and enhancing strength.
    • Functional Training Area: This space is designed for bodyweight exercises, kettlebells, resistance bands, and more, to improve flexibility, coordination, and overall fitness.
    • Group Class Studio: A dedicated space for our group fitness classes, including yoga, HIIT, Zumba, and more, with a motivating environment.

  2. Equipment
    Velocity Fit is equipped with cutting-edge equipment to support all your fitness goals:

    • Cardio Machines: Latest models of treadmills, ellipticals, stationary bikes, and rowing machines for effective cardiovascular workouts.
    • Free Weights & Machines: A wide selection of dumbbells, barbells, and machines to help you increase strength and endurance.
    • Functional Training Gear: Kettlebells, resistance bands, and medicine balls to support diverse training routines.
    • Smart Equipment: Innovative machines with digital tracking and app connectivity to monitor your progress.

  3. Special Features & Services
    Velocity Fit is more than just a gym – it's a complete wellness hub. Alongside our fitness areas and equipment, we offer several features to enhance your fitness journey:

    • Sauna: After an intense workout, relax and detox in our modern sauna, which promotes recovery and muscle relaxation.
    • Nutrition Counseling: Our certified nutrition experts offer personalized guidance to help you fuel your body for optimal performance and overall health.
    • Swimming Pool: Enjoy a refreshing swim in our indoor pool, perfect for recovery or low-impact cardio.
    • Massage Therapy: Take advantage of our post-workout massage services to ease muscle tension and aid recovery.
