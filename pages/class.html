Fitness Programs

Whether you're looking to build strength, improve flexibility, or increase endurance, we have something for everyone. Below is a list of our exciting classes, along with their details:

  1. Cardio Training
    Class Duration: 45 minutes
    Schedule: Monday, Wednesday, Friday - 6:00 AM & 7:30 PM
    Cardio Training is an energizing workout that focuses on improving heart health, stamina, and overall fitness. This class includes exercises like cycling, running, and high-intensity interval training (HIIT).

    • Benefits: Improves cardiovascular health, burns calories, and boosts endurance.
    • Instructor: Ron, Certified strength and conditioning coach.

  2. Strength Training
    Class Duration: 60 minutes
    Schedule: Tuesday, Thursday - 8:00 AM & 6:30 PM
    This class focuses on building muscle strength and improving overall body composition using weights and resistance exercises.

    • Benefits: Increases muscle mass, enhances metabolism, and promotes bone health.
    • Instructor: Ron, Certified strength and conditioning coach.

  3. Yoga & Flexibility
    Class Duration: 60 minutes
    Schedule: Monday, Wednesday - 5:30 AM & 7:00 PM
    Yoga is designed to increase flexibility, reduce stress, and improve mental clarity. Our sessions incorporate various poses, breathing exercises, and mindfulness techniques.

    • Benefits: Improves flexibility, promotes relaxation, and enhances mind-body connection.
    • Instructor: Ayra, Yoga and mindfulness expert.

  4. High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)
    Class Duration: 30 minutes
    Schedule: Monday, Thursday - 6:00 PM
    HIIT classes are designed to push your limits by alternating between intense bursts of exercise and short recovery periods. It’s a great way to burn fat and increase endurance in a short amount of time.

    • Benefits: Boosts metabolism, increases strength, and burns fat rapidly.
    • Instructor: Gautam, HIIT and endurance specialist.

  5. Pilates
    Class Duration: 45 minutes
    Schedule: Tuesday, Friday - 7:30 AM & 5:00 PM
    Pilates focuses on core strength, flexibility, and overall body alignment. This low-impact class is perfect for anyone looking to improve posture and prevent injuries.

    • Benefits: Strengthens core muscles, improves posture, and enhances flexibility.
    • Instructor: Ayra, Yoga and mindfulness expert.

  6. Zumba
    Class Duration: 45 minutes
    Schedule: Wednesday, Saturday - 8:00 AM & 5:30 PM
    Zumba is a high-energy dance class that combines fun music with exciting dance moves. It’s a great way to improve cardiovascular fitness while having a blast.

    • Benefits: Boosts cardiovascular health, burns calories, and improves coordination.
    • Instructor: Gautam, HIIT and endurance specialist.
